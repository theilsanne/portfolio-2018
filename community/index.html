Community

I wouldn't be where I am without the support of the design community, so I've been making sure to give back wherever and however I can. I'm available for mentorship, so if I can help you with anything please reach out to me!

I'm an alumni mentor for New Media Club, and a mentor for Radify Labs' online community.

 

I've given talks on presentation types, prototyping (with Abbey Lee), my internship, and building and presenting portfolios (with Olivia Grace).

 

I talked to Sahil for Students Who Design, Georgie for Hey Girlfriend!, and for a student AMA with Radify.

 

I wrote about my first internship for Grayscale, and a post-graduation reflection for myself.

 

I was an ADAA double-semifinalist in 2017, and an ADAA double-semifinalist and winner in 2018.

 

My work has been recognised on Cofolios and on Bestfolios.
